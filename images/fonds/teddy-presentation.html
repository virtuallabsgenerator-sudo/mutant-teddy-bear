📖 Histoire du Teddy

This is the extraordinary story of Teddy Bear Land, attacked by the Dark Teddy Bears with their powerful magic. Their land is on the brink of destruction because of their dark magic. The greatest shadow wizard has cast a spell on Teddy Bear Land, transforming them into mutants. They must fight to save their country! Their mutation has given them powers they will use to defeat the Dark Teddy Bear army. But the wizard has more than one trick up his sleeve: he has summoned the Monster Teddy Bears, whom they must face at the risk of their lives. A wise Teddy Bear told the story of the Teddy Bears of Light who lived in the celestial kingdom. Perhaps they will help them win? The adventure begins, choose your side! Adventures await you in Mutant Teddy Bear Land.
